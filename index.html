ABOUT

Whether you're a seasoned expert or just beginning your journey in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, our vibrant community welcomes you. The AI & ML Club is a student-led initiative committed to exploring the transformative potential of AI and ML across diverse domains such as healthcare, finance, and education. Through interactive workshops, seminars with industry professionals, and hands-on projects, we empower students to deepen their technical knowledge, solve real-world challenges, and drive innovation. Together, we aim to shape the future of technology while fostering collaboration and a passion for AI and ML.

Our mission is to educate, inspire, and support students in pursuing their passion for AI and ML, providing a platform to grow, innovate, and make meaningful contributions. Be a part of something extraordinary—join us today!

Glimpses of some of the great events carried out by TAM-VIT

CODECORTEX

CodeCortex was a 42-hour hackathon bringing together tech enthusiasts to develop innovative solutions across blockchain, development, IoT, cybersecurity, AI/ML and robotics. Teams collaborated to create interdisciplinary prototypes addressing real-world challenges.

Algoarena, a 34-hour AI/ML hackathon at VIT Vellore's YANTRA fest, challenged student teams to solve real-world problems with innovative solutions. Participants showcased cutting-edge projects, with top teams winning prizes for their creativity and impact.

ALGOARENA

DATA ALCHEMY 2.0

The AI and ML Club at VIT Vellore hosted Data Alchemy 2.0, a 12-hour machine learning workshop combining theory with six hands-on projects. The event focused on practical implementation of ML concepts, providing participants with real-world experience in various machine learning applications.

Survival Showdown was a team-based escape room challenge that tested participants' problem-solving and collaboration skills. Teams competed to be the first to complete interconnected tasks, combining strategic thinking with quick decision-making in an adrenaline-fueled environment.

FAQs

How to join TAM-VIT?

Every year, VIT students are given the opportunity to enroll in various clubs. When the enrollment period begins, students will receive an email notification. At that time, they can select the 'AI&ML Club' and click 'Final Submission' to complete the enrollment process.

What are the perks to join the club?

Joining the AI&ML Club at VIT comes with numerous perks. Members will get the experience of organizing events and workshops. They will also get to work on various low-level and high-level projects. Additionally, members will get to interact with seniors and alumni, allowing them to strengthen their professional network. Furthermore, the club will provide opportunities to explore many more domains beyond just AI and machine learning.

Does the club limits upto AI&ML only?

The AI & ML Club spans diverse domains - Technical, Management, Design, Outreach, and Editorial. Technical subdomains include IoT, Frontend, Backend, MLOps, Open Source, and AI & ML. Club projects leverage cross-disciplinary expertise.

Are there any prerequisites required to enroll in the club?

There are no prerequisites to join the AI & ML Club. Whether you are a complete beginner, intermediate, or advanced in the field of AI, the club welcomes you. The club will cater to members across skill levels, ensuring everyone can explore and evolve within the dynamic world of artificial intelligence.

How to participate in projects cycles?

The AI & ML Club will circulate a Google form to gather project ideas from members. Top 50 ideas will be shortlisted, and you'll work on them in teams, guided by the board. No prerequisites to join - the club welcomes beginners, intermediates, and advanced learners alike, catering to all skill levels in the dynamic world of AI.
